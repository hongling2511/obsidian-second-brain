# Agentic AI: A Comprehensive Study Guide

This study guide provides an overview of the fundamental advances in artificial intelligence known as Agentic AI, based on the insights provided during the NVIDIA GTC 2025 Keynote. It explores the definition, capabilities, and practical applications of AI systems that possess agency.

---

## Core Concepts Overview

Agentic AI represents a significant evolution in artificial intelligence, moving beyond simple data processing toward systems that possess "agency." Unlike traditional models, Agentic AI is characterized by its ability to interact with its environment and execute complex tasks autonomously.

### The Five Pillars of Agentic AI
According to the source context, Agentic AI is defined by five core functional capabilities:

1.  **Contextual Perception:** The ability to perceive and understand the specific context of a circumstance.
2.  **Reasoning:** A critical advancement where the AI can reason about how to approach and solve a specific problem.
3.  **Planning:** The capacity to formulate a strategic plan of action to achieve a desired outcome.
4.  **Action:** The ability to take physical or digital actions based on its reasoning and planning.
5.  **Tool Utilization:** The capacity to leverage external tools and multimodal information to complete tasks.

### Multimodality and Learning
Agentic AI is built to understand multimodal information, meaning it can process various types of data simultaneously, including text and video. This allow the AI to interact with the digital world in a human-like manner, such as:
*   Navigating websites and understanding their format.
*   Synthesizing information from written words and visual media (playing and learning from videos).
*   Integrating newfound knowledge into its existing framework to perform its designated job more effectively.

## Short-Answer Practice Questions

**1. What is the fundamental characteristic that distinguishes "Agentic AI" from previous iterations of artificial intelligence?**
*   **Answer:** The defining characteristic is "agency," which allows the AI to perceive context, reason about problems, plan actions, and execute those actions using tools.

**2. According to the transcript, what is the importance of reasoning in Agentic AI?**
*   **Answer:** Reasoning is described as a very important advancement that allows the AI to determine the specific logic and steps required to answer a question or solve a problem.

**3. How does Agentic AI interact with websites to acquire new knowledge?**
*   **Answer:** The AI can visit a website, analyze its format, read the words, and play/watch videos to learn. It then uses this "newfound knowledge" to complete its job.

**4. What role does multimodality play in the functionality of an AI agent?**
*   **Answer:** Multimodality allows the AI to understand and process different types of information (such as text and video) simultaneously, which is essential for using tools and navigating complex digital environments like websites.

## Glossary of Key Terms

| Term | Definition |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Agency** | The capacity of an AI to act independently, perceive its environment, and make decisions to achieve a goal. |
| **Agentic AI** | An advanced form of AI that possesses agency, enabling it to understand context, reason, plan, and take action. |
| **Contextual Perception** | The ability of an AI to interpret the specific circumstances or environment surrounding a task. |
| **Multimodality** | The ability of an AI system to process and understand multiple types of data inputs, such as text, images, and video, simultaneously. |
| **Planning** | The cognitive-like process of an AI determining a sequence of steps or actions required to solve a problem or complete a task. |
| **Reasoning** | The logical process used by an AI to evaluate how to solve a problem or provide an answer based on available information. |
| **Tool Utilization** | The capability of an AI to access and use external resources (e.g., websites, software, or media) to facilitate its work. |
